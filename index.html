Advantages of Truecaller

Advantage 1

Caller Identification: Truecaller allows you to identify incoming calls from unknown numbers by displaying the caller's name and photo if available in its extensive database. This feature helps you decide whether to answer the call or not, enhancing your call screening process.

Advantage 2

Spam Call Blocking: Truecaller has a built-in spam call blocking feature that automatically identifies and blocks unwanted spam calls and telemarketing calls. This helps you avoid the annoyance of spam calls and ensures that your phone is not cluttered with unwanted calls..

Advantage 3

Contact Management: Truecaller offers robust contact management capabilities. It enables you to keep your contacts organized by allowing you to merge duplicate contacts, edit contact information, and even send contact requests to friends and acquaintances. This simplifies your contact list and ensures you have up-to-date information.
